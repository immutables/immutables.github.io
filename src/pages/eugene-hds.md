Worked on RoboFS at HDS ([hdsglobal.com](https://hdsglobal.com)). Architecting an execution system for next generation touch-free
fulfillment centers. The system orchestrating and coordinating work of custom-built robotic cells: automated
induction, storage (ASRS), robotic picking and packaging, and autonomous mobile robots (AMR).
The system was put together in our Technology Center at Plainfield, IA, demonstrating seamless integration of the
hardware and software components. It was shown to our investors and interested companies, including Ingram
Micro, Toyota, Amazon.
