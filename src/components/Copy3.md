**Feature Packed.** Many ways to handle attributes: compute [derived](/immutable.html#derived-attributes), [lazy](/immutable.html#lazy-attributes) and [default](/immutable.html#default-attributes) values; exclude from equality, or [redact](/immutable.html#redacted) from `toString`. [Nullable](/immutable.html#nullable), [Optional](/immutable.html#optional-attributes) and [Collection](/immutable.html#collection) attributes.
_Immutables_ are [serialization](/immutable.html#serialization) ready. [JSON](/json.html) integration via [Jackson](/json.html#jackson) (v2 and v3), and [Gson](/json.html#generating-type-adapters) libraries.
